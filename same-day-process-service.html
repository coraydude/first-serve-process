Why evening attempts complete faster

Most failed serves fail because attempts happen when nobody’s home. Same-day dispatch pushes attempts into the after-work window, when contact rates are highest.

That scheduling advantage — plus GPS-verified documentation — is why same-day jobs have our highest first-attempt completion rate.

Same-day service questions

What’s the cutoff for same-day service?

2pm local time for guaranteed same-day attempts in major metros. Documents received later are attempted first thing the next morning.

Is same-day service available everywhere in Florida?

Guaranteed in major metro counties (Hillsborough, Miami-Dade, Orange, Duval, Pinellas, Broward, Palm Beach and more). In rural counties we quote realistic same- or next-day timing before you commit.

Do you serve evenings and weekends?

Yes. Florida law permits service any day except Sunday (and legal holidays for some documents); we routinely serve evenings and Saturdays.

What proof do I get the same day?

A completion notice with GPS and time details the same evening, followed by the notarized affidavit of service — typically delivered the next business morning.
