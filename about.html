Our Story

Four decades in the legal field — now serving Central Florida

First Serve Process Solutions wasn't started by someone new to law. It was founded by a legal professional with 40 years of experience working inside the field — as a paralegal and litigation-support specialist alongside attorneys, courts, and clerks across Florida.

That's the difference you feel on the very first serve. After four decades of preparing filings, chasing down deadlines, and watching cases stall on nothing more than bad service, our founder built the firm around the one thing attorneys said they never had enough of: a process server they never had to chase for a status update.

Today we cover all 67 Florida counties with court-appointed servers in every judicial circuit — anchored in Orlando and Central Florida, and reaching Tampa Bay, South Florida, and Jacksonville. We added skip tracing and mobile notary teams and a vetted nationwide network — while keeping a single dispatcher as your one point of contact.

Our mission: keep your case moving. Every serve, filing, and locate exists to protect someone's deadline — and after 40 years in this world, we treat it that way.
