About First Serve Process Solutions

Fifteen years of licensed, court-compliant process serving across Florida — built on speed, transparency, and affidavits that hold up.

Our story

First Serve began in 2011 with one server, one county, and a simple observation: attorneys didn't need another process server — they needed one they never had to chase for status. So we built the firm around communication: confirmed receipt in 15 minutes, GPS-stamped attempts, and updates before you think to ask.

Fifteen years and 40,000+ serves later, that's still the whole playbook. We've grown to cover all 67 Florida counties with court-appointed servers in every judicial circuit, added skip tracing, court filing, and mobile notary teams, and built a vetted nationwide network — while keeping a single dispatcher as your one point of contact.

Our mission: keep your case moving. Every serve, filing, and locate exists to protect someone's deadline — we treat it that way.
