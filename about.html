About First Serve Process Solutions — Licensed Florida Process Servers

Experienced, court-compliant process serving across all 67 Florida counties — built on fast communication, GPS-verified attempts, and affidavits that hold up in court.

Our story: process serving built for Florida attorneys

First Serve started with one server, one county, and a simple observation: attorneys didn't need another process server — they needed one they never had to chase for a status update. So we built the firm around communication: confirmed receipt on intake, GPS-stamped attempts, and updates before you think to ask.

That's still the whole playbook. We've grown to cover all 67 Florida counties with court-appointed servers in every judicial circuit — from Tampa and St. Petersburg across the Bay to Orlando in Central Florida, Miami and Fort Lauderdale in the south, and Jacksonville in the north. Along the way we added skip tracing and mobile notary teams, and built a vetted nationwide network — while keeping a single dispatcher as your one point of contact.

Our mission: keep your case moving. Every serve, filing, and locate exists to protect someone's deadline — we treat it that way.
