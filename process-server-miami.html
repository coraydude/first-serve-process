Miami process serving questions

How fast can you serve papers in Miami?

Because our servers are local to Miami-Dade County, most orders get a first attempt the same or next business day. Documents received by the afternoon cutoff can be attempted same-day, and rush orders across Brickell, Coral Gables, Doral and Hialeah typically fall well inside a 24–48 hour window.

Which courthouse handles Miami cases?

Most Miami civil matters are filed with the Miami-Dade County Courthouse under the 11th Judicial Circuit, located on NW 1st St in downtown Miami. Every serve we complete in Miami-Dade is documented with a notarized, court-compliant affidavit that meets that court's requirements.

Do you serve evasive defendants in Miami?

Yes. For a defendant avoiding service in high-rise Brickell buildings, gated Coral Gables communities or anywhere in Miami-Dade, we combine attempts at varied times with skip tracing and, where warranted, substitute service consistent with Florida Statutes Chapter 48 — documenting each attempt so the record supports your case.
