Licensed process serving in Orlando, Tampa, Miami & every Florida county

First Serve Process Solutions is a licensed Florida process-serving company built for attorneys, law firms, and self-represented litigants who need service done right the first time. From service of a summons or subpoena to skip tracing an evasive defendant, our servers work every day to keep your case moving — deadlines met, defendants located, and court-ready proof in hand.

Every serve is completed under Florida Statutes Chapter 48 and documented with a notarized affidavit, or return of service, so the record holds up in court. Where the law requires it, we effect substitute service correctly and log GPS-verified attempts, giving you clean due-diligence when a defendant is hard to reach. When a routine attempt fails, we escalate to skip tracing and stakeout service rather than returning your papers unserved.

Statewide coverage across Florida — plus nationwide reach

Our Florida process servers cover all 67 counties, including the Tampa Bay area, Miami-Dade, Broward, Orlando and Central Florida, Jacksonville, St. Petersburg, and Fort Lauderdale. Standard attempts across our core metros typically begin within 24–48 hours, and rush and same-day options are available when a filing deadline is close. Need service outside Florida? Our nationwide network extends coverage to all 50 states through vetted, licensed servers, all managed from a single point of contact.
