Court filing questions

Can you file in any Florida county?

Yes — we e-file statewide through the Florida Courts E-Filing Portal and have runners for physical filings in every judicial circuit.

How fast are filings confirmed?

E-filings are submitted within hours of receipt and you get the portal confirmation the same day. Walk-in filings return stamped copies the same or next business day.

Do you handle filing fees?

Yes. We advance clerk fees and itemize them on your invoice, or use your firm's portal account if you prefer.

Can you file my return of service automatically?

Yes — bundle filing with process serving and every affidavit is filed with the court as soon as it's notarized, with confirmation sent to you.
