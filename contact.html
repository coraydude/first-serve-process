First Serve Process Solutions provides licensed service of process in every corner of Florida — from Hillsborough and Pinellas in Tampa Bay, to Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach in South Florida, to Orange County in Central Florida and Duval County in Jacksonville. Contact & Quote FAQ

How fast can a Florida process server pick up my documents?

During business hours we confirm receipt and dispatch quickly. For standard service across Florida, expect the first attempt within a typical window, and for rush or same-day requests we can often make first contact within 24–48 hours. If you're on a deadline, call (407) 697-4530 and a dispatcher will prioritize your job.

Which Florida counties do you serve process in?

All 67 Florida counties. That includes major metros such as Tampa, Miami, Orlando, Jacksonville, St. Petersburg, and Fort Lauderdale, plus rural and coastal communities statewide. For legal documents beyond Florida, we coordinate nationwide service through our partner network in all 50 states.

How much does process serving cost in Florida?

Pricing depends on the county, the urgency (standard, rush, or same-day), and whether the defendant is difficult to locate. Submit the form above or call us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your service and location — there's no charge to ask.

What if the defendant is evasive or the address is bad?

Evasive defendants are routine work for us. We combine persistent, well-timed attempts with skip tracing to locate current addresses, and where the law allows we pursue substitute service — always documented in a court-compliant affidavit under Florida Statutes Chapter 48. Tell us what you know in the form and we'll take it from there.

Can you handle rush or same-day service of process?

Yes. Rush and same-day service is one of our core offerings for attorneys and self-represented litigants facing tight court deadlines. Choose "Same-day" in the urgency field or call our monitored rush line, and we'll move immediately across the Tampa Bay, Central Florida, and South Florida metros.
