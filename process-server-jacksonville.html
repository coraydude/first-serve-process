Jacksonville process serving questions

How fast can you serve in Jacksonville?

Within Jacksonville and Duval County, rush orders are typically attempted within a few hours during business hours, and same-day service is available when documents arrive by the afternoon cutoff. Because our servers are local to the First Coast — Downtown, Riverside, Southside, Mandarin and the Beaches — first attempts happen fast, well inside a 24–48 hour window.

Which courthouse handles Jacksonville cases?

Most Jacksonville and Duval County matters are filed at the Duval County Courthouse on N Pearl St in Downtown Jacksonville, which houses the 4th Judicial Circuit. We serve and document to that circuit's standards, and each completed serve comes with a notarized, court-compliant affidavit and GPS-verified proof of the attempt.

Do you serve evasive defendants in Jacksonville?

Yes. For defendants avoiding service anywhere from San Marco to the Beaches, we combine attempts at varied times of day with skip tracing and, where warranted, substitute service consistent with Florida Statutes Chapter 48. Every attempt is GPS-verified and documented so the record supports your case.
