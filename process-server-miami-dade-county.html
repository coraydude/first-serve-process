Process serving built for Miami-Dade County

Miami-Dade is Florida's most populous county, and serving it well means knowing it block by block. Our servers work the whole map — the high-rise corridors of downtown Miami and Brickell, the gated communities of Coral Gables and Aventura, the industrial parks of Doral and Hialeah, and the sprawling residential grids of Kendall and Homestead. When a defendant lives behind a guard gate in Coconut Grove or works a shift in the Miami Beach hospitality district, we already know how to reach them.

We serve summons and complaints, subpoenas, eviction and unlawful-detainer papers, family-law filings and small-claims documents to the standards of Florida Statutes Chapter 48, and every completed serve comes back with a notarized, court-compliant affidavit that holds up before the 11th Circuit.

Every attempt is GPS-verified and reported to you in real time.

Miami-Dade County process serving questions

How fast can you serve in Miami-Dade County?

Because our servers are local to Miami-Dade, most jobs are attempted the same or next business day. Documents received by the afternoon cutoff can get a same-day attempt anywhere from downtown Miami and Hialeah to Kendall and Homestead — typically well inside a 24–48 hour window.

Which courthouse handles Miami-Dade cases?

Miami-Dade civil, family and eviction matters are heard in the 11th Judicial Circuit, centered on the Miami-Dade County Courthouse and its adjacent civil and family divisions in downtown Miami. Every return of service we file is a notarized, court-compliant affidavit that meets the 11th Circuit's standards.

Do you serve evasive defendants in Miami-Dade?

Yes. In a county with millions of residents and constant movement between Miami Beach, Doral, Aventura and Homestead, evasion is common. We combine attempts at varied times of day with skip tracing and, where warranted, substitute service consistent with Florida Statutes Chapter 48 — documenting each attempt so the record supports your case.

How much does a process server cost in Miami-Dade County?

Pricing depends on the service address, how many defendants are on the papers, and whether you need standard, rush or same-day handling. We quote the full amount up front before you commit — no surprise fees. Request a quote for your exact Miami-Dade job.

Are you local to Miami-Dade, or a statewide call center?

Both, in the best way. First Serve is local to Miami-Dade County with servers who know the neighborhoods and courthouse — and we also cover all 67 Florida counties. If your case reaches into Broward, Orlando or Central Florida, the same team keeps the file moving without a handoff.
