Nationwide service questions

How long does out-of-state service take?

Typically 3–7 business days for a first attempt depending on the state and locality, with rush options in most major metros. We confirm expected timing when we quote.

Whose rules apply to an out-of-state serve?

Generally the destination state’s rules govern how service is performed, while your court’s rules govern the proof required. We manage both sides so the affidavit works everywhere it needs to.

Can you serve a registered agent in another state?

Yes — corporate and registered-agent service is among our most common nationwide work, including bulk campaigns across multiple states.

Do you handle international service?

We coordinate service abroad under the Hague Service Convention and other applicable channels. International timelines run months, not days — contact us early in your case.
