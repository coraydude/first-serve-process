Mobile notary questions

How quickly can a notary reach me?

Same-day appointments are usually available in major Florida metros; most appointments are scheduled within 24 hours statewide.

What ID does the signer need?

A current government-issued photo ID (driver license, passport, or state ID). Florida also permits credible witness identification in limited cases — ask us if ID is an issue.

Can you notarize for someone in a hospital or facility?

Yes — we do bedside signings regularly. The signer must be alert, aware, and signing willingly; we coordinate timing with family and facility staff.

Do you offer remote online notarization (RON)?

Yes. Florida authorizes RON, and our online notaries can complete many notarizations by secure video with identity proofing — useful for out-of-state signers.
