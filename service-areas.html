Coverage questions

Is service slower in rural counties?

First attempts in metro areas happen within 24–48 hours; rural counties typically add a day. Rush options exist statewide — we quote realistic timing for your exact address before you commit.

Do you charge travel fees?

Our flat quotes are all-in for the address you give us. Remote addresses may carry a higher flat fee, but it's stated up front — never a surprise mileage line.

What about serves outside Florida?

Same dispatcher, same process: we route out-of-state documents through our vetted nationwide network and return proof of service that satisfies both jurisdictions.
