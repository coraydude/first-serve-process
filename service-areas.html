Florida coverage questions

Is process serving slower in rural Florida counties?

First attempts in metro areas like Orlando, Tampa, Miami, and Jacksonville typically happen within 24–48 hours; rural and remote counties usually add a day. Rush and same-day options are available statewide, and we quote realistic timing for your exact address before you commit.

Do you charge travel fees for remote addresses?

Our flat quotes are all-in for the address you give us. Remote or hard-to-reach addresses may carry a higher flat fee, but it's stated up front — never a surprise mileage line after the fact.

Which Florida counties do you cover?

All 67 of them. Our court-appointed servers work across every judicial circuit in the state, from the Panhandle and North Florida through Central Florida, Tampa Bay, and South Florida down to the Keys. If an address exists in Florida, we can serve it.

Can you serve an evasive defendant who moves around the state?

Yes. We combine skip tracing with local, region-based servers to locate and serve evasive defendants across county lines. When personal service isn't possible, we pursue court-compliant substitute service under Florida Statutes Chapter 48 and document every attempt.

What about serves outside Florida?

Same dispatcher, same process. We route out-of-state documents through our vetted nationwide network across all 50 states and return proof of service that satisfies both jurisdictions.
