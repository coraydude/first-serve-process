Orlando process serving questions

How fast can you serve papers in Orlando?

Because our servers are local to Orlando and Orange County, first attempts are often made within hours during business hours. Same-day service is available when documents arrive by the afternoon cutoff, and nearly every Orlando serve is attempted well inside a 24–48 hour window.

Which courthouse handles Orlando cases?

Orlando and Orange County cases are handled by the Ninth Judicial Circuit and filed at the Orange County Courthouse on N Orange Ave in Downtown Orlando. Every serve we complete comes with a court-compliant, notarized affidavit that meets Ninth Circuit filing requirements.

Do you serve evasive defendants in Orlando?

Yes. For defendants avoiding service anywhere in Orange County — from Dr. Phillips to the Milk District — we vary attempt times, add skip tracing and stakeout options, and use substitute service where warranted under Florida Statutes Chapter 48. Every attempt is GPS-verified so the record supports your case.
